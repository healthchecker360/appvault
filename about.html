About AppVault

Your trusted source for free Android APK and Windows software downloads across South Asia.

What is AppVault?

AppVault is a free download platform designed specifically for users in Pakistan, India, and South Asia. We provide safe, direct download links for Android APK files and Windows software — including old versions that are no longer available on official stores.

Many users need older versions of apps for compatibility reasons, or simply prefer an older interface they are used to. AppVault makes it easy to find and download any version you need, without registration, without payments, without complications.

Our Mission

We believe software should be accessible to everyone. AppVault was built to serve the millions of users across South Asia who want reliable access to apps and software without barriers.

Everything on AppVault is completely free. We do not sell any software, and we only list apps that are freely available. Our download links point to trusted third-party storage services — we do not host any files on our own servers.
