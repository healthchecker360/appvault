Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

AppVault ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

AppVault does not require registration or accounts. We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any personal information.

Like all websites, our hosting provider (Vercel) automatically collects basic server logs including your IP address, browser type, and pages visited. This is standard practice and we have no control over it. This data is used only for security and performance monitoring.

Cookies & Advertising

We display advertisements through Google AdSense and PropellerAds. These ad networks may use cookies to show you relevant ads based on your browsing history. This is controlled by Google and PropellerAds, not by AppVault. You can opt out of personalized ads through Google's Ad Settings.

Download Links

When you click a download link on AppVault, you are redirected to a third-party storage service (such as TeraBox or Mega). These services have their own privacy policies. AppVault is not responsible for data collected by those services.

Analytics

We may use basic analytics tools to understand how many people visit our site and which pages are popular. This data is aggregated and anonymous — we cannot identify individual users from this data.

Google Sheets

Our app database is stored in Google Sheets. This data is publicly readable and contains only app information (names, descriptions, download links) — no personal user data is stored there.

Children's Privacy

AppVault is a general-audience website and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has submitted personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please visit our contact page.
