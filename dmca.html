DMCA Policy

AppVault respects intellectual property rights and responds promptly to valid copyright concerns.

Our Position

AppVault does not host, store, or distribute any files directly. All download links on this website point to third-party storage services such as TeraBox, Mega, or Google Drive. AppVault functions as a directory and index of publicly available download links.

We only list apps that are free to download. We do not list paid apps, cracked software, or modified versions that violate developer terms of service.

Copyright Infringement Notice

If you believe that content listed on AppVault infringes on your copyright, please send a DMCA notice containing the following information:

  • Your full legal name and contact information
  • The specific URL(s) on AppVault that you believe infringe your copyright
  • A description of the copyrighted work you claim has been infringed
  • A statement that you have a good faith belief that the use is not authorized by the copyright owner
  • A statement that the information in the notice is accurate and that you are the copyright owner or authorized to act on their behalf
  • Your electronic or physical signature

How to Submit

Send your DMCA notice to us via our contact page. Select "DMCA / Copyright issue" as the subject. We will review your notice and take action within 24-48 hours.

Our Response

Upon receiving a valid DMCA notice, we will remove the infringing listing from AppVault as quickly as possible. If the content is hosted by a third-party service, we will also attempt to notify that service.

Counter-Notice

If you believe content was removed by mistake or misidentification, you may submit a counter-notice. Counter-notices must include your contact information, identification of the removed content, a statement under penalty of perjury that you have a good faith belief the content was removed by mistake, and your consent to jurisdiction.
